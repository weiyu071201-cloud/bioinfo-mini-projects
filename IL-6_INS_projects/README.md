In the 5'-UTR of mouse insulin 1 (Ins1), there are both a start codon and a stop codon. The code was designed to select the longest CDS. A line chart comparing the insulin gene (INS) and IL-6 gene was generated, and their standard deviations were calculated. The results show that among the four species (human, mouse, rat, and cow), the INS gene exhibits a greater fluctuation in GC content.
